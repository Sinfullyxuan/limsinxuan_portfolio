ABSTRACT

This project explores the creation of a graphic pattern inspired by a meaningful movie or song quote. By translating words into visual elements, the design captures the emotional tone, themes, and atmosphere of the chosen quote, transforming narrative into pattern through composition, colour, and form.

This choosen quote is below

“If you completely forget your name, you will never find your way home”.

— Haku | Spirited Away

Interpretation + Elements Designed

Time

Forget - what we cherish now becomes tomorrow’s memory

Showcasing how time moves forward, regardless of our awareness

Teddy Bears

Name - holds memory, history, and meaning of ourselves and loved ones

Portrayed as teddy bears, a companion when we are young and in the present

Home

Home - found through love and connection

Home where people feel the most comfortable and safe
