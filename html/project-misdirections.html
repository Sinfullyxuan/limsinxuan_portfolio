ABSTRACT

The intention of our interactive piece is to encourage players to trust their instincts while experiencing the emotional states one may encounter when navigating life. Through movement and choice, players progress through a series of forest zones, each representing a different emotion associated with searching for direction.

As players move forward, the environment transitions both visually and sonically, with changes in atmosphere reinforcing each emotional state. The use of evolving audio enhances immersion and emotional resonance. Experienced through the Oculus Quest 2 VR headset, the surrounding visuals fully envelop the player’s field of view, creating a deeply immersive and introspective experience.

Design Intent

Forest of Misdirections is an immersive VR experience that invites players to trust their instincts while navigating a surreal forest filled with uncertainty. Dense fog and arrow-shaped leaves pointing in conflicting directions create hesitation, making the first step forward crucial.

As players move through the forest, the environment shifts into different zones that reflect emotional states encountered when searching for direction in life. Each path offers a new possibility, ultimately questioning whether there is ever a single “right” direction to follow.

Three States

Blue Zone

Blue zone symbolizes the feelings of sadness and helplessness when lost.

Uses Blue Arrows, Gloomy Lighting, Rain, Concrete Floor

Red Zone

Red zone symbolizes the confusion and frustration of being lost

Uses Red Arrows, Thick Fog, Falling Leaves, Rocky Floor

Green Zone

Green zone symbolizes the relief and clarity after finding a right direction.

Uses Green Arrows, Light Fog, Butterflies, Flowers
